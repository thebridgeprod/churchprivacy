Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how The Bridge ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with our Production Team SMS Assistant — an internal messaging tool that helps our production team manage service schedules and volunteer coordination.

This service is intended exclusively for internal use by authorized production team staff of The Bridge.

Information We Collect

When you interact with our SMS assistant, we may process the following:

  • Your mobile phone number — used to route messages and restrict access to authorized users only.
  • Message content — the text of messages you send and receive via this service.
  • Conversation history — kept temporarily in memory to provide context across a session. This is cleared when the server restarts or when you send the word RESET.

We do not collect names, email addresses, or any other personal information beyond what is listed above.

How We Use This Information

Information is used solely to operate the SMS assistant, including to:

  • Process your messages and generate AI-assisted responses
  • Retrieve scheduling and volunteer data from Planning Center on your behalf
  • Restrict access to authorized phone numbers only

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose unrelated to operating this internal tool.

Third-Party Services

This service relies on the following third-party providers to function. Each has its own privacy practices:

  • Twilio — provides SMS delivery infrastructure. Messages pass through Twilio's platform. See twilio.com/legal/privacy.
  • Anthropic — provides the AI (Claude) that generates responses. Message content is sent to Anthropic's API for processing. See anthropic.com/legal/privacy.
  • Planning Center — provides church scheduling and volunteer data. We access Planning Center on your behalf using a Personal Access Token. See planningcenter.com/privacy.
  • Railway — hosts the server that processes messages. See railway.app/legal/privacy.

Data Retention

Conversation history is stored in server memory only and is not written to a database or permanent storage. It is automatically cleared when the server restarts or when you send the command RESET.

Twilio and Anthropic may retain message logs according to their own policies. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any third parties except the service providers listed above, which are necessary to operate the SMS assistant. We do not share your information for advertising or any commercial purpose.

SMS Messaging Terms

To opt out: Reply STOP to any message at any time. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent.

For help: Reply HELP or contact us at the email below.

Message frequency varies based on your usage. Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

This service is for internal church production team use only. Access is restricted to pre-authorized phone numbers.

Security

Access to this service is restricted to a single authorized phone number. Messages are transmitted over encrypted connections. We take reasonable steps to protect the information processed through this service, though no system can guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

This service is intended for adult staff members only and is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
